Driving directions

We are located at 7000 Rossmore Lane in El Dorado Hills’ Town Center – on the hill above the movie theatre. Please see driving directions below.

From Hwy 50 East Bound:

  1. Take the Latrobe Rd exit and turn right off the exit.
  2. Make a left on White Rock Rd.
  3. Make a left on Vine St.
  4. Make a right on Rossmore Lane just BEFORE the movie theatre parking lot.

From Hwy 50 West Bound:

  1. Take the El Dorado Hills/Latrobe Rd exit and turn left onto Latrobe Rd
  2. Make a left on White Rock Rd.
  3. Make a left on Vine St.
  4. Make a right on Rossmore Lane just BEFORE the movie theatre parking lot.

Tuxes? Check!
No Comments

Two weekends ago Bobby (Joshua’s biological brother) and Amber finally announced their wedding date: July 18th. In other words a little over a month from now. Everything from invitations to tuxes had to be done at warp speed. This afternoon Joshua and I met met up with them and two other groomsmen to get fitted for their tuxes. Meanwhile, I looked through the binder to get the tuxes for our wedding picked out.

Joshua will be wearing a turquoise vest and tie along with two button black jacket. As for the groomsmen and fathers, they’ll be wearing the same only their vests and ties will be brown.

This brown will also be the color of the bridesmaid dresses, except for Dani’s; her dress will be the same turquoise as Joshua since she is the Maid of Honor. Since we have yet to pick the fabric out for the dresses (my bridesmaid Serena is making the dresses), I figured it would be easier to find fabric to match the colors Men’s Wearhouse has available rather than the other way around. Now all I need to do is get cracking on the dress shopping!

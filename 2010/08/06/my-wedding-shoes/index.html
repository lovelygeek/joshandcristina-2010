My Wedding Shoes!
4 Comments

They just arrived, fresh from Barneys, my shoes for the wedding! I decided to splurge on the shoes since I got a fabulous deal on my dress. I also decided to go with silver so I can wear them on more than one occasion – ivory doesn’t really go with much.

The ultimate single gal, Cristina Assenza, will be married in Manolos to Diamond Springs mechanic Joshua Martin Robinson come fall.

Yes, I know, waaay cheesy, but I just had to throw that in there. Afterall, I’m a big (no pun intended) Sex & the City fan!

Anyway, I tried them on along with my dress and veil, and all I can say is I am SO excited! I cannot wait to get married!
